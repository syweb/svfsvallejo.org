Junior High (Grades 6-8)

In the junior high, heavy emphasis is placed on acquiring student skills in preparation for the rigors of high school.

Staff and Faculty — Our junior high program is departmentalized with teachers specializing in various arts and sciences. However, each grade has a homeroom teacher who is ultimately responsible for the class.

Core Curriculum — Coursework that is taught every day includes Language Arts, Mathematics, Social Studies, Religion and Science.

Enriched Curriculum — Junior High students have regular instruction in Computers and Art. In addition, students choose one elective from a variety of programs.
