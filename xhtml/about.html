About Us

  • Founded and served by the Dominican Sisters of San Rafael since 1868.
  • Fully accredited by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC) and the Western Catholic Education Association (WCEA).
  • Safe, secure campus and high expectations for student conduct.
  • We teach as Christ did: the Gospel values of love, justice, forgiveness, and service to all.
  • Before and after-school child care available right on campus – operating every day that school is in session from 6:45am – 6:00pm.
  • An educational mission of Saint Vincent Ferrer Catholic Parish and open to students of all creeds and backgrounds.
  • Strong Catholic faith formation including Sacramental preparation for First Communion, Reconciliation, and Confirmation.
  • Participate in Solano/Pittsburg Athletic League (SPAL) Sports program for Boys and Girls.
  • Innovative pre-school program, providing high caliber private Catholic School preparation.
