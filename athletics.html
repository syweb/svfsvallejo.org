Athletic Program

The Athletic Program consists of intramural sports and cheerleading. Cheerleading is reserved for 7th and 8th grade girls, baseball for 7th and 8th grade boys, volleyball for girls in grades 5-8, boys’ basketball for grades 5-8 and girls’ basketball for grades 5-8.

The Solano/Pittsburg Athletic League

During the school year 1999-2000, the seven schools participating in intramural sports formed the Solano/Pittsburg Athletic League and, with the help of the Catholic School Department:

  • Created the SPAL Commission to develop policies and mediate problem resolution. Members of the Commission include principals, athletic directors and a chair.
  • Developed a Mission Statement and a set of Objectives, and
  • Instituted general rules/guidelines for participating students, parents and coaches.
