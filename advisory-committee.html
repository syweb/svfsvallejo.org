CSAC

The Catholic School Advisory Commission (CSAC) is a policymaking and advisory commission consistent with and supportive of the policies of our Bishop, our Pastor and the Diocesan School Board. The general purpose of this commission is to support the philosophy of the school, whose aim is to integrate the teachings of the Gospel values with the total curriculum, and then to create an environment where Christian community can be experienced through liturgy, sacraments and special actions.

Meetings are open to members of the parish and all parents and legal guardians of students in the school, and are held on the third Tuesday of each month at 7 p.m. in the Learning Center unless otherwise specified in the school calendar, or by a special vote of the Commission. Agenda items should be submitted to the principal or CSAC President ten (10) days ahead of the meetings.
