Student Council

St. Vincent Ferrer School student government provides elected student offers the opportunity to exercise leadership in student affairs.

The Student Council sponsors various activities and event to help build school spirit and to provide funds for officers to attend the Catholic Association of Student Council leadership workshops, for contributions to various school programs and for charitable donations.
