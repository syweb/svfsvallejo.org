Elementary (Grades 1-5)

Staff and Faculty — St. Vincent Ferrer School has a core teacher for each of its grade levels, as well as an instructional aide.

Core Curriculum — In grades One through Five, St. Vincent Ferrer School concentrates on providing students with the basic skills of reading, writing, grammar, spelling, mathematics, religion, science and social studies. Emphasis is placed on the acquisition of knowledge and the development of learning skills. The school program follows the Diocesan Standards which meet or exceed California State Standards.

csdsac.org/curriculum.htm

Enriched Curriculum — Students in grades One through Five have regular instruction in Physical Education, computer, music and library.
